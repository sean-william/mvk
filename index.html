Company Profile

Established in 2003, MV Lien Minh Ltd Co., is the exclusive agency of Leonhard Kurz Stiftung & Co. KG in the Vietnamese market. KURZ is the worldwide leader in thin film technology.

Kurz decorative foils and functional solutions can be found on almost everything around us, from packaging, books, greeting cards, wallets, shoes, automotive components, tickets, cellphones, washing machines, textiles, bottle labels to bank cards. The application possibilities are virtually endless.

As a part of the worldwide KURZ network of more than 9 manufacturing plants, 15 subsidiaries and over 80 exclusive agencies, we have our head office in Ho Chi Minh City.

Today, we serve over 500 companies of all size and profiles in various industries across Vietnam.

Our Products

Hot Stamping Foils

Hot stamping foil has the best reflective finish in comparison with other coating techniques. The application process is dry, quick and environmentally friendly. It is also highly versatile as it can be used on different substrates.

For an even more impressive visual impact, hot foils can also be profiled or embossed to add tactile quality to the hot foil stamped area with no additional process required.

Our hot foils are available in a wide range of standard shiny metallic/matte tones, pigment, white, transparent, holographic and diffraction patterns.

Cold Foils

Cold foiling has higher speed of application and greater flexibility when creating designs. Unlike hot foil stamping, the process of cold foil transfer involves applying adhesive onto the image area without the need of using dies. After the foil has been in contact with the specific area, the adhesive is cured by UV light and then the carrier film is removed.

Despite hot foils having a wider range of standard colours available, custom colours are made possible by overprinting base gold, silver, diffractive or holographic cold foils.

Holographic Foils

Cold foils are also available in holographic designs. Large-area diffractive structures or single images can be selected. Besides shiny metallic foils, there are also a range of transparent holographic foils to choose from.

Stamping Tools

Our dies are highly precise, easy to use and able to produce sharp contour stampings, all of which that you would need to impress your audience.

Transform your design ideas into unique stampings and benefit from a variety of dies, relief styles and pattern options with our dies and tools!

Thermal-Transfer Ribbons & Coding Foils

Thermal-transfer ink ribbons are also called thermal transfer foils, carbon ribbons, and TTF or TTR (thermal-transfer ribbon).

Depending on the purpose or project requirements, the correct type of ink ribbons should be used for the best results. Different type of surface to be printed, the durability and security requirements of the print are some important considerations when choosing the correct thermal-transfer ink ribbon.

TRUSTCONCEPT®

TRUSTCONCEPT® is the combination of our TRUSTSEAL® foil technologies and TRUSTCODE® digital solutions into appealing and complete packages to decorate, protect against counterfeits, and to communicate key market information of your product.

Linking visual effects to information technology opens new opportunities for branding and market analysis - genuine added value for you and your customers. Brand protection is becoming ever more important. As a leading provider of brand security we offer you a quick and easy way for identification via smartphone.

Card Technology & Application Machines

Kurz is one of the leading suppliers of security technologies and components for cards. Our expertise revolves around credit, bank, payment, customer, loyalty, prepaid cards, and also ID documents such as identification cards or driving licenses.

The card component that we supply includes, Magnetic Foils, Signature Foils, Holographic OVDs, Tipping Foils, Cold Indent Foils, and also Scratch-Off Foils.

We also supply application machines that meet a diverse range of card requirements.
